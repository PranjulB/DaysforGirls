Our Mission and Vision

Days for Girls' To empower menstruators of all ages and backgrounds through good menstrual hygiene practices by breaking the silence about menstruation, to spread awareness about menstrual hygiene, mobilizing volunteers, usage and disposal of menstrual products and innovating sustainable solutions that shatter stigmas and limitations for women and girls. And to provide products to as many individuals as possible. Every Girl. Everywhere. Period.
